About Us

We are a small operation that grew from a 40 year love for cool cars and trucks into a hobby - and now into a specialised business. I can honestly say that I only buy trucks that I would be happy keeping and driving myself (and some of them I have).
We have over 10 years experience in importing vehicles and have a genuine passion for finding good trucks to suit most budgets and desires. We typically seek out rust free big block V8 classic Ford trucks from 1967 - 1990 but there have been some exceptions and we are always on the look-out for quality trucks of any age.
We are happy to assist buyers with pathways to historic or full registration and/or advice on the requirements in your state.
